## What changed during this edition's construction

The main editorial discovery was in Phase 1: the survey process was US-biased in its first pass. The correction — running explicitly international queries — produced Morocco/France as a finalist that wouldn't have appeared otherwise. The OS note is recorded: balance US and international coverage from the start of Phase 1.

The edition itself came together without major structural revision, which is new. Previous editions required 2-3 rounds of construction revision. The nine-element check at Checkpoint 1 may be doing more of the work that revision previously had to do — by the time construction begins, the structure is already mostly right.
